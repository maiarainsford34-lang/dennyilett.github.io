About

A Musician'sMusician

Denny Ilett is one of the UK's most versatile and accomplished musicians — a self-taught guitarist, vocalist, bandleader, composer, arranger and teacher whose musical explorations span jazz, blues, big band, swing, rock, soul, funk and psychedelia.

Based in Henley-on-Thames, Oxfordshire, Denny has spent over four decades performing and recording at the highest level. He has collaborated with a remarkable range of musicians including funk legend Pee Wee Ellis, conductor Charles Hazlewood, Will Gregory (Goldfrapp), Adrian Utley (Portishead), soul artist Lewis Taylor, Paul Jones (Manfred Mann), trumpet virtuoso Bobby Shew, and celebrated New Orleans vocalist Lillian Boutté — official musical ambassador for the city of New Orleans, and only the second artist to hold that title after Louis Armstrong — with whom he toured globally for over two decades as musical director.

He is also deeply involved in education, teaching jazz guitar privately in Berkshire and South Oxfordshire and online to students worldwide, and is a respected writer and regular contributor to Guitarist Magazine, Guitar World, MusicRadar and UK Jazz News.

Guitarist & Arranger

Guitarist &Arranger

A self-taught guitarist with a voice entirely his own, Denny's playing draws on the deepest roots of jazz, blues and beyond. His intricate chords, smooth melodies and improvisational style bring a fresh perspective to classic and contemporary material — single-note lines, jazz comping, big band writing and beyond, always in service of the music, never at its expense.

The Sinatra
Experience

Denny Ilett is one of the most celebrated Sinatra interpreters working today — renowned for his smooth, charismatic delivery and deep knowledge of the Great American Songbook. His acclaimed Frank show has delighted audiences from Europe to Australia, performing at premier venues and festivals across the globe.

Denny writes and arranges all his own charts for the show — bringing a musician's ear and arranger's craft to every performance. Whether fronting a full 16-piece band or an intimate combo, every performance is a masterclass in capturing Sinatra's warmth, wit and unrivalled style while adding his own unmistakable artistic flair.

Available for weddings, corporate events, festivals, jazz clubs and private functions throughout the UK and internationally — Berkshire, Oxfordshire, Hampshire, Surrey, London and beyond.

Jazz GuitarLessons & Masterclasses

Denny Ilett offers jazz guitar lessons from his base in Henley-on-Thames, making him ideally placed to teach students across Berkshire, South Oxfordshire and the surrounding area — including Reading, Wallingford, Didcot, Marlow, Maidenhead and beyond. Online lessons available worldwide.

With over 40 years of professional performing experience, Denny is a patient, inspiring and highly knowledgeable teacher. His Jazz Demystification approach strips away the theory and puts the music first — fretboard logic, real improvisation, and the stylistic detail that separates a jazz guitarist from someone who just knows the scales. Lessons cover jazz harmony, chord theory, improvisation, jazz standards, blues technique, fingerpicking, sight-reading, composition and arranging.

Whether you are a complete beginner, an intermediate player looking to develop your jazz vocabulary, or an advanced musician seeking to refine your improvisation and arrangement skills, Denny tailors each lesson to your individual goals. All levels welcome.
