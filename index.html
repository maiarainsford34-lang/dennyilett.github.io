About

A Musician'sMusician

Denny Ilett is one of the UK's most versatile and accomplished musicians — a self-taught guitarist, vocalist, bandleader, composer, arranger and teacher whose musical explorations span jazz, blues, big band, swing, rock, soul, funk and psychedelia.

Based in Henley-on-Thames, Oxfordshire, Denny has spent over four decades performing and recording at the highest level. He has collaborated with a remarkable range of musicians including funk legend Pee Wee Ellis, conductor Charles Hazlewood, and celebrated New Orleans vocalist Lillian Boutté — with whom he toured globally for over two decades as musical director.

Guitarist

Guitarist &Arranger

A self-taught guitarist with a voice entirely his own, Denny's playing draws on the deepest roots of jazz and blues. His intricate chords and smooth melodies bring a fresh perspective to classic material.

As an arranger, Denny is one of the most versatile in the UK. His orchestral work extends beyond jazz — creating large-scale arrangements that seek the unexpected connection between musical worlds.
