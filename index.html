Denny Ilett is one of the UK’s most versatile and accomplished figures in contemporary music. A self-taught guitarist, vocalist, and educator, Denny’s career is defined by a rare ability to inhabit diverse musical worlds—from the grit of New Orleans blues to the sophisticated arrangements of the big band era—with effortless authenticity.

Based in Henley-on-Thames, Oxfordshire, Denny has spent over four decades performing and recording at the highest level. His musical explorations span jazz, blues, big band, swing, rock, soul, funk and psychedelia.

He has collaborated with a remarkable range of musicians including funk legend Pee Wee Ellis, conductor Charles Hazlewood, and celebrated New Orleans vocalist Lillian Boutté — with whom he toured globally for over two decades as musical director.

As the co-founder and Artistic Director of the Bristol International Jazz & Blues Festival, Denny continues to be a central pillar of the UK music community, bridging the gap between the technical and the soulful.

Vocalist

The Sinatra Experience

Denny Ilett is one of the UK's most celebrated jazz vocalists, renowned for his smooth, charismatic interpretations of the Great American Songbook. His acclaimed Frank Sinatra tribute show has delighted audiences at festivals across the South of England and beyond.

Whether fronting a full 16-piece big band or a smaller combo, every performance is a masterclass in swing—capturing Sinatra's warmth while adding his own unmistakable artistic flair. Denny creates lush, rhythmically driven charts that honour tradition while speaking to contemporary audiences.

The Guitarist

A self-taught guitarist with a voice entirely his own, Denny's playing draws on the deepest roots of jazz and blues. His intricate chords and smooth melodies bring a fresh perspective to everything from Gypsy Jazz to heavy rock.

The Arranger

One of the UK's most sought-after arrangers, Denny meticulously crafts his own Frank Sinatra big band charts and orchestrates the complex, psychedelic textures of the Electric Lady Big Band.
