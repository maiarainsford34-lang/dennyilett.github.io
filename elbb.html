The Concept

Hendrix as you'venever heard him

The Electric Lady Big Band is a 16-piece jazz orchestra led by guitarist, vocalist and arranger Denny Ilett — reimagining the music of Jimi Hendrix through the lens of jazz, big band, soul and psychedelia.

Since its founding in 2018, the band has become one of the most celebrated live acts in the UK — with thirty-two sold-out performances at Ronnie Scott's Jazz Club.
