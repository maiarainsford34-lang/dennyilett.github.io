Denny Ilett is one of the UK’s most versatile figures in contemporary music. A self-taught guitarist and vocalist, his career spans from New Orleans blues to sophisticated big band arrangements with effortless authenticity.

He has collaborated with funk legend Pee Wee Ellis and celebrated vocalist Lillian Boutté, touring globally for over two decades as a musical director and soloist.
