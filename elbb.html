The Concept

Hendrix as you'venever heard him

The Electric Lady Big Band is a 16-piece jazz orchestra led by guitarist, vocalist and arranger Denny Ilett — reimagining the music of Jimi Hendrix through the lens of jazz, big band, soul and psychedelia. Every arrangement is written by Denny, drawing on decades of experience to honour Hendrix's genius while creating something entirely new.

Since its founding in 2018, the band has become one of the most celebrated live acts in the UK — with thirty-two sold-out performances at Ronnie Scott's Jazz Club and appearances at Cheltenham Jazz Festival, among many others. The project has produced two studio albums.

Live

An explosivelive experience

The Electric Lady Big Band delivers a live experience that is unlike anything else on the UK jazz scene. Brass, rhythm, guitar and vocals combine to create a wall of sound that fills any room — from intimate jazz clubs to major festival stages.

The band interprets Hendrix's Electric Ladyland album in full, plus tracks from across his catalogue — all reimagined as big band arrangements that reveal new depths in music already considered some of the greatest ever written.

Denny leads the band from downstage, conducting, playing and singing — an arranger and performer at the top of his craft.

Flexibleconfigurations

The Electric Lady Big Band can be booked in several configurations to suit your venue, budget and production requirements.

Full Orchestra
16-piece
The complete Electric Lady Big Band — all brass, rhythm section, guitar and vocals. Ideal for major festivals, arts centres and theatres.
Core Band
10-piece
A streamlined version of the band retaining the full sonic impact. Perfect for jazz clubs and mid-size venues.
Hybrid
Flexible
Denny brings a core group and supplements with locally hired musicians reading his charts — a key selling point for international touring and overseas festivals.
